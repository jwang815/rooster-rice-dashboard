Key Takeaways

What's Going Well

  • Revenue surged 21.6% vs P2 to $604K, driven by delivery growth and catering expansion
  • Stanyan achieved highest margin (18.7%) with lowest labor cost (21.1%)
  • Q1 2026 revenue $1.51M vs $1.31M prior year (+15.3%), net profit up 15.9%
  • 3P delivery fee savings of $70.5K YoY through direct ordering shift

Areas Needing Attention

  • Marina net loss of -$2,618 (-3.8%) driven by 38.5% labor cost — highest in chain
  • Stonestown loss of -$3,112 (-3.1%) with 34.7% labor and 11.9% occupancy drag
  • Marketing spend surged to $19,649 (3.3% of sales) — up from $8,954 PY; ROI unclear
  • Santa Clara food cost at 25.0% — highest across locations

Bottom Line

P3 net profit of $41K (6.8%) is the strongest period this year. Delivery (52% of sales) remains the engine. Priority actions: restructure Marina staffing, evaluate Stonestown lease terms, and audit marketing ROI. Q1 tracking at $63K net profit — on pace for a strong year.
